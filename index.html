S/01 - About

Behind every beautiful product is a quieter system. I build the system.

Four years ago I walked into Andela's nine-month full-stack program in Kigali and walked out shipping production code. Since then I've worked across two continents - from an AR product team in Rwanda to an AEM engineering org in Milan - and the lesson is the same: software is a contract with the business, not with the framework.

I work end-to-end. Architecture, API, frontend, mobile, deploy. Java in the morning, Flutter in the afternoon, the case-for-not-building-it memo at the end of the week. Currently lead engineer on three live products serving real users in production.
